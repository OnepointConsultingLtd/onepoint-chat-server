## Sangeetha Viswanathan

### Head of Delivery and Operations

### Head of Delivery and Operations

- 

As a PRINCE2-certified project manager – one of the most highly regarded certifications in the world – Sangeetha helps to oversee implementation of Onepoint client projects.

Sangeetha’s range and experience bring added perspective to Onepoint – she has lived and worked in wildly different markets across three continents, project managing in the automotive sector, and working in skills development.

Fluent in Japanese, a Chemistry grad and Computer Applications Masters, Sangeetha joined Onepoint in 2012 as a Business Analyst, a role which has since evolved to encompass all operations.

But her skills don’t stop when she leaves the office, not only is Sangeetha a green belt in Kung-Fu, she is an Indian classical dancer and regularly performs at local charity events alongside her teenage daughter.
